The Process

How It Works

Simple to start. From sign-up to first session in under 24 hours.

Step 01

Sign Up & Intake Form

Fill in the sign-up form with your sport, schedule, goals, training background, and injury history.

Step 02

Alan Reviews Your Profile

Your details are reviewed within 24 hours — sport demands, competition schedule, training age, physical profile.

Step 03

First Programme via TrainHeroic

Your first week's programme lands in TrainHeroic. Every exercise has instructions, sets, reps, and load targets.

Step 04

Train & Get Feedback

Complete your sessions and message Alan on WhatsApp with questions, form clips, or updates. One reply per day guaranteed.

Step 05

New Programme Each Week

Every week, a fresh programme adapts to your previous sessions, upcoming fixtures, and progress.
