Client Agreement

Read the Atkins Performance Coaching Agreement

1. Services

The Coach agrees to provide strength and conditioning coaching services as outlined in the agreed package. This may include online programming, movement assessments, and progress reviews.

2. Health Declaration

The Client confirms they have disclosed any pre-existing medical conditions, injuries, or medications that may affect their ability to train. The Client agrees to inform the Coach of any changes to their health status.

3. Assumption of Risk

The Client acknowledges that physical training carries inherent risk of injury. The Client participates voluntarily and accepts responsibility for their own wellbeing during and between sessions.

4. Payment Terms

Payment is due before the start of each membership period. Late payments may incur a suspension of services.

5. Cancellation Policy

1-month memberships can be cancelled at any time with 30 days' notice. 6-month and 12-month memberships are committed for the full term.

6. Refund Policy

Memberships are non-refundable once started, except in cases of injury (with medical evidence) where a pro-rata credit may be offered at the Coach's discretion.

7. Confidentiality

The Coach will treat all client information as confidential. Training data, health information, and personal details will not be shared with third parties without written consent.

8. Intellectual Property

Training programmes provided are for the Client's personal use only and may not be shared, distributed, or resold.

9. Liability

The Coach holds valid professional indemnity and public liability insurance. The Coach's liability is limited to the value of the services purchased.
